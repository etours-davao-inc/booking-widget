Davao City Countryside and Shopping Tour

Last Updated: 08/22/2019

3 days

Explore Metropolitan Davao by visiting its downtown area and it?s Countryside. Wrap your stay with a 3 hour shopping tour in Davao?s to-go-to shopping sites.

Itinerary

Suggested arrival in Davao: Before 12:00nn

Suggested departure from Davao: 2 pm onwards

DAY 1 - AIRPORT PICK-UP AND CITY TOUR (6 HOURS)

  • Ponce Gallery Suites
  • Lon Wa Buddhist Temple
  • Chinatown And Magsaysay Fruit Market
  • San Pedro Cathedral
  • Bone Collectors Museum (not available on Sunday)
  • Running tour of Central 911
  • Duterte's House
  • Jack's Ridge

  • Malagos Garden Resort
    • Malagos Chocolate Museum

  • Matina Town Square
  • Durian candy Factory Outlet
  • Fruit Market
  • Citra Mina Frozen Tuna Products
  • Aldevinco Shopping Center
  • Orchid Selling Area
  • Chocolate selling area
  • Airport transfers
